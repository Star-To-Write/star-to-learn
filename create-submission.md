# Creating a New Submission

How to upload and create a new Star to Write submission in Sanity, our submissions manager.

or watch the video [here](https://youtu.be/4ILOtx0eRlw)

## Step 1

Navigate to [www.sanity.io/@or44Qy8Sg/studio/hr7su3z3ar0fipdfnazrg3ci/default/structure/submission](https://www.sanity.io/@or44Qy8Sg/studio/hr7su3z3ar0fipdfnazrg3ci/default/structure/submission)

## Step 2

Click on "Submissions"

![Step 2](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_e38c13ba-381c-48e3-bc87-3f27aad4883d.png)

## Step 3

Click 'Create new document' to start creating a submission

![Step 3](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_f9dd83b2-f06f-4bd2-a0c6-82f6b9edb037.png)

## Step 4

Click the 'Title' field to create a title. Do not press the top Untitled part.

![Step 4](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_60c61eb9-c8ce-4c74-a8b0-0fd5830f1551.png)

## Step 5

Type the title.

## Step 6

Click on "Generate" to create the link for the submission. If it doesn't look good, you can fix it.

![Step 6](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_8114b86a-a78b-4edf-9aea-2c2cc413685a.png)

## Step 7

Click here to find an author.

![Step 7](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_b868d86a-7063-4dae-bd4d-26253691c322.png)

## Step 8

Type the author name.

## Step 9

If there are no results, click on "Create" to create a new Author profile.

![Step 9](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_15db309a-11fd-48ad-bad5-7c9c8f072957.png)

## Step 10

To create a new author, write the preferred name of the author.

![Step 10](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_d0c68b0f-262a-469a-93f3-9a4b6de02dbf.png)

## Step 11

Add the author's email.

![Step 11](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_813a837c-5cfd-410e-a7c0-df888f3841bf.png)

## Step 12

If the author is anonymous, click on "Publish Anonymously"

![Step 12](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_eafc305e-e76a-40c0-90b3-8773dc1492a9.png)

## Step 13

Click on "Publish" to create a new Author profile.

![Step 13](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_0b2e8bbf-2996-4bd7-80b5-fe6b806a1210.png)

## Step 14

Close the newly-created author profile to return to your submission.

![Step 14](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_045b8274-f950-4b73-ba49-5686a195c4a4.png)

## Step 15

Navigate to [www.sanity.io/@or44Qy8Sg/studio/hr7su3z3ar0fipdfnazrg3ci/default/structure/submission;72389d5d-60a4-488b-b522-6740aebc78cc%2Ctemplate%3Dsubmission](https://www.sanity.io/@or44Qy8Sg/studio/hr7su3z3ar0fipdfnazrg3ci/default/structure/submission;72389d5d-60a4-488b-b522-6740aebc78cc%2Ctemplate%3Dsubmission)

## Step 16

Write a small excerpt of the title. This can be the first few sentences of the submission or a small summary.

![Step 16](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_33ee2223-1843-4d01-b83c-4db496e1b5d2.png)

## Step 17

Type the excerpt.

## Step 18

Click here to find a category.

![Step 18](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_b6ea216f-d0dc-4b15-8ab1-13909727a2d2.png)

## Step 19

You can see all categories by pressing the dropdown button here.

![Step 19](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_73c77bb6-dd2d-4ae5-b577-965ccabeeae1.png)

## Step 20

Click on a category.

![Step 20](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_2ec0faff-80a6-4a4c-802c-d8f48f803dd7.png)

## Step 21

To add the images posted on social media, click on "Add Item".

![Step 21](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_cac62195-3401-4bc5-bad9-4749442811a3.png)

## Step 22

Upload the first image.

![Step 22](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_c34a5e3b-456e-46c5-b944-044a1ba40266.png)

## Step 23

Finish editing your image if needed.

![Step 23](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_cf2f2eca-1bbc-404e-980a-4a2b0c7b2879.png)

## Step 24

Click on "Add item" to add another photo.

![Step 24](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_e42e5fee-b3f6-47a1-8a0c-54b5a45e6d21.png)

## Step 25

Upload your image.

![Step 25](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_856d147e-1b61-4d26-a4df-a12dff3a16f4.png)

## Step 26

Click here to finish.

![Step 26](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_5be3a29f-094d-4b21-a5c1-358516cb5510.png)

## Step 27

If needed to reorder, drag the image to reorder it.

![Step 27](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_7fd38248-32f8-411c-a5a9-23bf51240d07.png)

## Step 28

To add the content of the submission, click on the content field.

![Step 28](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_f1d5d553-93c9-4ac0-a3c7-9240846f068e.png)

## Step 29

Type in the content.

## Step 30

To style, highlight the section that requires styling.

![Step 30](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_68c70023-7d88-4a95-b158-1da0a3e3bda8.png)

## Step 31

Click on "Normal" to decide on the alignment of the content.

![Step 31](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_b73ff5dd-2fe8-433e-8cd5-7e142ee3df7e.png)

## Step 32

Click on "Center" to center the text.

![Step 32](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_f7954569-d603-4222-8c11-67b69558b061.png)

## Step 33

Click on a part of the text you wish to bold or italicize.

![Step 33](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_bbc56d94-9962-4a97-a160-02b2b483d9df.png)

## Step 34

To add a tag, click on "Add Item".

![Step 34](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_30928533-5569-4b57-ad17-9efca9356f9c.png)

## Step 35

Click here to type in a tag.

![Step 35](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_08e0c575-e97f-4111-b472-9e1a99af31ed.png)

## Step 36

Feel free to choose a pre-existing tag.

![Step 36](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_ec9d5276-7cdb-4263-b90a-1b3f6a669f27.png)

## Step 37

Click on a tag.

![Step 37](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_aa587f1b-ad6b-4ae6-b3b6-9e4626d45287.png)

## Step 38

Click on "Publish" to finalize your submission.

![Step 38](https://trails.nyc3.digitaloceanspaces.com/annotated_screenshot/WzNaaa0ZWDK3_46b059ae-4f43-4f9d-8ce3-1f0b3b14bb50.png)
